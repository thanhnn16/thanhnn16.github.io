La Vela Saigon Hotel

280 Nam Ky Khoi Nghia, Ward 8, District 3, District 3, Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam – Great location

Get the celebrity treatment with world-class service at La Vela Saigon Hotel

Situated in Ho Chi Minh City, 700 m from Tan Dinh Market, La Vela Saigon Hotel features accommodation with an outdoor swimming pool, free private parking, a fitness centre and a terrace. Among the facilities of this property are a restaurant, room service and a 24-hour front desk, along with free WiFi throughout the property. Some units at the property have a balcony with a city view.

Selected rooms also boast a kitchen with a fridge and a minibar.

A buffet, à la carte or Italian breakfast can be enjoyed at the property.

War Remnants Museum is 1.6 km from the hotel, while Reunification Palace is 1.9 km from the property. The nearest airport is Tan Son Nhat International Airport, 5 km from La Vela Saigon Hotel.
